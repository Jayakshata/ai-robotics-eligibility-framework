How the lab is organised

1 · Foundation Lab — computers + a few starter robotsWhere everyone learns first, mostly on simulation (a robot on a screen). Cheap, and scales to big classes.
2 · Specialisation Labs — the real robots & factory gearWhere small groups go deep on real hardware. The expensive industrial arms — BGR Neo already owns.
The money rule: software is free, the money goes on computers, and the big robots are already ours.
