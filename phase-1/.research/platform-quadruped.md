## 1. Definition & Scope
A **four-legged, floating-base mobile robot** — the form factor that trades the wheel's efficiency for **discrete-contact traversal of unstructured terrain** (stairs, rubble, catwalks, gravel, confined spaces). What makes it distinct as an engineering target: unlike a fixed arm (bounded, deterministic) or an AMR (planar, continuous contact), the quadruped is an **underactuated, dynamically-balancing system that makes and breaks contact** — the whole engineering problem is keeping a floating base upright while feet intermittently touch unknown ground. Balance and terrain are *primary*, not incidental. It is the first legged form factor to reach real commercial deployment (inspection is the killer app), and it is the **proving ground for learned locomotion** — the RL-in-sim → zero-shot-real pipeline matured here before humanoids. Two sub-forms: **pure legged** (max terrain, ~1–1.5 m/s cruise) and **wheeled-legged** (wheels on feet — speed on flat + legs for obstacles, e.g. Unitree B2-W, ANYbotics ANYmal-on-wheels research).

## 2. Signature Capability Stack
- **A5 Legged Locomotion** *(load-bearing — the platform's defining problem)* — gait generation (trot/bound/crawl/stair), dynamic balance, contact scheduling, fall recovery. → `locomotion`
- **A6 RL / Learned Control** *(the dominant modern paradigm)* — end-to-end neural locomotion policies trained in massively-parallel sim, deployed zero-shot; now beats hand-tuned MPC on rough terrain. → `reinforcement-learning-control`
- **A1 Rough-Terrain Perception + Sensor Fusion** — elevation/height-mapping, traversability, LiDAR + depth + proprioception fusion for perceptive locomotion. → `computer-vision-3d-perception`, `multimodal-sensor-fusion`
- **A3 Navigation** — autonomous waypoint/mission nav over 3-D uneven terrain, stair and gap negotiation, obstacle avoidance. → `motion-planning-navigation`
- **A2 State Estimation / SLAM** — legged-inertial + LiDAR-inertial odometry (LIO), contact-aided estimation of a floating base without GPS indoors. → `state-estimation-slam`
- **A4 MPC / Whole-Body Control** *(the model-based baseline still in production)* — convex-MPC + whole-body QP for balance and force distribution. → `control-classical-optimal-mpc`, `whole-body-compliant-control`
- **A8 Sim-to-Real & Simulation** *(the enabling loop)* — domain randomization, terrain curricula, actuator-net identification. → `sim-to-real-transfer`, `simulation-digital-twins`
- **A5 Loco-Manipulation** *(emerging brain)* — arm-on-back or leg-as-manipulator; door-opening, valve-turning. → `manipulation-and-grasping`

## 3. Integration & Platform-Specific Skills
The "integration tax" no single capability owns: **dynamic-balance control of a floating underactuated base** (capture point / ZMP-free contact reasoning); **gait design and contact scheduling**; **actuator-net / sim-to-real identification** for backdrivable QDD joints; **perceptive-locomotion pipelines** (real-time elevation mapping fused into the policy); **legged state estimation** without external reference; **fall detection + self-righting**; **payload-on-the-back integration** (inspection sensor turrets, thermal/acoustic/gas, or a manipulator arm — shifting CoM); and the field-robotics reality of **ruggedization & environmental certification** — IP67/IP68 washdown, and the platform's hardest bar, **ATEX Zone 1 / IECEx Ex-rating** for explosive atmospheres. Plus **fleet-autonomy / mission orchestration** and RaaS deployment.

## 4. Typical Verticals
**B14 inspection** — the native, dominant home (~45% of deployments): refineries, power/utilities, chemical plants, **data centers**, semiconductor fabs, offshore rigs; the quadruped walks fixed rounds reading gauges/thermal/acoustic where wheels can't climb. **B3 defense/security** — reconnaissance, perimeter patrol, EOD; prized for low acoustic signature + agility. **B7 mining** and **B6 construction** — progress monitoring, hazardous survey. **B8 oil & gas** — Ex-rated inspection. **B16 nuclear/hazmat** — teleop-heavy survey. Chosen because all need **mobility over human-built or broken terrain (stairs, ladders, catwalks) in dirty/dangerous settings** — exactly what legs unlock over wheels.

## 5. Eligibility Profile
The archetypal **"legged / quadruped robotics engineer"** bundles **locomotion + RL-learned control + rough-terrain perception + navigation + state estimation + sim-to-real**. Strongest backgrounds: **Robotics / ME / EE** for the embodiment side (floating-base dynamics, MPC/WBC, QDD actuation, contact mechanics); **CS / AI** for the learned-locomotion side (RL, perception, policy nets). Platform-specific must-haves: **RL-locomotion training in Isaac Lab / legged_gym**, **sim-to-real** (domain randomization, actuator nets), **MPC/whole-body QP**, **LiDAR-inertial odometry**, **ROS 2**, and field-deployment/ruggedization literacy. Feeder capability profiles: `locomotion`, `reinforcement-learning-control`, `sim-to-real-transfer`, `state-estimation-slam`, `motion-planning-navigation`, `control-classical-optimal-mpc`. Tiers: **L1** field deployer/fleet operator → **L2–L3** locomotion + perception + nav software → **L4/L5** learned-locomotion / loco-manipulation research. The platform premium: a **real robot walking robustly on hardware over unseen terrain** (sim demos are cheap; sim-to-real on legs is the differentiator).

## 6. Platform-Specific Hardware & Stack `~`
**Robots:** **Boston Dynamics Spot** (1,500+ deployed, broadest payload ecosystem, Spot Arm) · **ANYbotics ANYmal D / ANYmal X** (X = world's first Ex-certified/ATEX-Zone-1 legged robot, 2026) · **Unitree Go2 / B2 / B2-W (wheeled) / A2 "Stellar Hunter"** (volume leader, open SDK) · **DEEP Robotics X30 / Lite3** (IP67 industrial) · **Ghost Robotics Vision 60** (defense). **Research:** **MIT Mini Cheetah / Cheetah 3** (Sangbae Kim), ETH **ANYmal**, **Stanford Doggo** (open-source). **Actuators:** **Quasi-Direct-Drive (QDD)** — low single-stage reduction (~5:1–25:1), backdrivable, proprioceptive torque via FOC (MIT-Cheetah lineage; CubeMars, cycloidal-QDD); Western platforms use higher-reduction gearboxes for precision. **Compute:** NVIDIA **Jetson Orin** on-board. **Sensors:** 360° LiDAR (Ouster/Velodyne/Chinese), depth cameras, ToF, IMU, joint torque/contact. **Software:** **ROS 2**; **NVIDIA Isaac Lab** + **Isaac/legged_gym** (GPU-parallel RL), **NVIDIA Newton** physics engine (GA at GTC 2026, w/ DeepMind + Disney), **MuJoCo**, **Genesis**; PPO training; ETH-RSL open-source locomotion stacks; **elevation_mapping_cupy**. **Autonomy/fleet vendors:** **Field AI** (field foundation models, L2 autonomy), **Skild AI**, **Formant** (fleet telemetry), **Anduril Lattice**, Levatas, Chironix.

## 7. Trending & Notable `⏱ as of mid-2026`
**Learned locomotion is now the default** — Isaac Lab pipelines give zero-shot sim-to-real; **RAI Institute open-sourced an end-to-end Spot training pipeline** (evolutionary sim-to-real, running to **5.2 m/s**). **NVIDIA Newton** physics engine reached GA (GTC 2026). **Cross-embodiment single policies** (ManyQuadrupeds — one policy across morphologies). **Loco-manipulation** is the active frontier: arm-on-back (Spot Arm, ReLIC interlimb-coordination RL), leg-as-gripper (**LocoMan**), ego-vision open-world (**SigLoMa**); **whole-body parkour / agile** work (CMU/Pathak). **Field-autonomy foundation models** (Field AI $400M+, Skild AI ~$4.5B val). **ANYmal X** Ex-certified market entry (2026). Market: **$3.12B (2025) → ~$17.5B (2035), ~19% CAGR**; ~25k units deployed; **Unitree ~70% of volume**. Caveat: **US/EU security restrictions** curb Chinese platforms in sensitive Western sectors (utilities, fabs, defense). *(Re-verify before publish.)*
